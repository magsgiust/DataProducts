March Madness Bracketology

Would you like to build the perfect bracket? Use this app to generate predictions of matchups you are curious about.

How it works

  1. Enter the matchup --Team A will be the first team (in alphabetic order) --Team B will be the last team (in alphabetic order) A very simple model is run predicting the winner between the two teams.

Next steps

  1. Select the box to see the favored team
  2. Select the box to see the team who actually won

Finally

  1. Click submit If the teams did not meet, you will see an "NA" result.
